    • about me





    My grandmother's first name is Fanny and her last name is Ben Amra. She was born on January 14, 1945 in Morocco. Her father's name was Maimon and her mother's name was Hanna, she grew up in Morocco and at the age of 11 she came to Israel by ship through Italy and came straight to Lod. My grandmother grew up in a school where they spoke French with a very strict education. She remembers that the teacher would beat them with her nails and give them very difficult tasks. As a child she used to play class and it's a shame. My grandfather and grandmother met through a joint company called Dorit, which created the first meeting between them, the rest of the meetings were held in a family setting, and after 5 months, as was customary at that time, my grandfather asked my grandmother's hand in marriage from her parents and a wedding date was immediately set. The wedding was held at CafÃ© Rivira in Lod, where a home-cooked meal was served with lots of joy and an Arab band. My grandmother was a seamstress and worked in a clothing store in her youth My grandmother's hobby is cooking and she cooks a lot of traditional Moroccan dishes.
